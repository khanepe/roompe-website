# Project Brief: RoomPe Website Rebuild

## Company Overview
**RoomPe** - Ahmedabad-based B2B student housing / proptech startup
- **Founded:** 2020 by Deval Patel & Satya Mehta
- **ARR:** $1 million (bootstrapped, no external funding)
- **Managed:** 300+ beds across 5 campuses in Western India (Gujarat)
- **Target:** Universities, B-schools (enterprise B2B)
- **Website:** https://roompe.co.in (currently down/parked)

## Services Offered
1. **Lease to Operate Model** - Universities lease property, RoomPe operates
2. **Management Contract Model** - RoomPe manages operations for institutions
3. **End-to-end hostel management:**
   - Room allocation
   - Food services
   - Laundry
   - Housekeeping
   - Security
   - Staffing
   - Technology/app

## Vision
>"Let universities and academicians focus on studies and student activities, while we take care of all daily hostel management from room allocation to check-out."

## Expansion Plans
- Cities: Rajkot, Vadodara, Surat, Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Bhavnagar
- Future: Residential schools, corporate accommodations

## Website Requirements
- **Type:** Enterprise-facing B2B website
- **Theme:** Claymorphism (modern, soft 3D UI elements)
- **Tech Stack:** Next.js, Tailwind CSS, React
- **Features:**
  - Scroll effects & animations
  - Interactive elements & micro-animations
  - Modern & beautiful design
  - Mobile responsive
  - Brand一致的视觉语言

## Claymorphism Design Direction
- Soft, raised elements with subtle shadows
- Light pastel backgrounds with glowing, puffy components
- Rounded corners, layered depth
- Smooth transitions and hover states
